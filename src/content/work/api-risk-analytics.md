## Background
Risk-model APIs offered a way to power analytics with live, standardized inputs. The
opportunity was to prove the concept end to end.

## What I Built
I created datasets by consuming **risk-model APIs** and built a **Power BI proof of concept**
that demonstrated how API-driven data could feed rich, scalable reporting.

## Outcome
- A working Power BI POC for API-driven analytics
- A reusable pattern for pulling standardized, API-sourced datasets
- A clear demonstration of scalable, modern reporting
