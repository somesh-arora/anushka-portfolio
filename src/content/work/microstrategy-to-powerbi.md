## Background
Health-system stakeholders relied on service-line dashboards for performance reporting, but the
legacy MicroStrategy implementation limited visual clarity and flexibility.

## What I Built
I rebuilt the service-line dashboards in **Power BI**, focusing on clean, effective visual
performance reporting, and **automated the benchmarking datasets in SQL** so executive reports
and member dashboards stayed current with far less manual effort.

## Outcome
- Modern, maintainable Power BI reporting for health systems
- Clearer visual storytelling of service-line performance
- Automated, SQL-driven benchmarking behind executive and member dashboards
