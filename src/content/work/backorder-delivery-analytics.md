## Background
Backorder management depended on knowing when items would actually arrive — but delivery-time
estimates weren't grounded in data.

## What I Built
I developed a **dataset and Tableau dashboard** providing **evidence-based estimated delivery
times**, and contributed to **SQL-based projects** that improved process efficiency and data
standardization.

## Outcome
- Evidence-based delivery-time estimates for better backorder decisions
- More efficient, standardized operational data
- A clearer, data-driven view for the team
