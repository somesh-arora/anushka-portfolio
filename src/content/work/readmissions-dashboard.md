## Background
Readmissions are a critical quality and cost signal. Clients needed an accessible way to see
outcomes and track the KPIs behind them.

## What I Built
I built a **Tableau readmissions dashboard** that turns clinical data into a clear outcomes-and-
KPI story for clients, and delivered **15+ member requests** translating clinical data into
actionable performance insights.

## Outcome
- A client-ready view of readmissions outcomes and KPIs
- 15+ member requests delivered with clinical performance insights
- Insights that fed directly into process-improvement work
